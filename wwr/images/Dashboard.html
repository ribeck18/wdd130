Financial Aid Determination Date (FADD)

Thursday, January 29 is FADD the Financial Aid Determination Date Links to an external site.

  • Your aid is about to be set in stone. After FADD, aid will not return if you drop an individual class (if you have participated). Classes added after FADD will not be eligible for aid.
  • If you drop a class after FADD, a W will land on your transcript. That could affect your SAP Status Links to an external site..

Feeling stressed? Come talk with the Financial Aid Office to see how the timing of dropping a class might affect your financial aid.

Come to Kimball 196, email financialaid@byui.edu, or live chat on BYU-Idaho webpages.
